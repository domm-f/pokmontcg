Bring the excitement of collecting Pokémon Trading Cards into Minecraft!

Open booster packs, collect cards, complete sets, trade with friends, and build your ultimate collection. The mod includes different card rarities, secret cards, advancements, and exciting pulls rates inspired by the real Pokémon Trading Card Game.

A brand-new set is released every Saturday!

The next set is chosen by community voting through Google Polls, giving players a chance to decide what expansion gets added next. Sometimes the winning set will be selected directly from community requests, while other weeks may be chosen by a random wheel spin.
[Google Form](https://forms.gle/4UwRXyqFAwQEHWhQ6)

How to get Pokémon TCG Products

A TCG Collector villager can be found in villages or be created by placing a chest near an unemployed villager. Once assigned, the TCG Collector offers a variety of Pokémon TCG products, including Booster Packs, Booster Bundles, and Elite Trainer Boxes that can be purchased through trading.

Binders

TCG Binders are now available through the TCG Collector villager. Binders now allow players to store, organize, and display their collections in customizable binders while tracking progress toward completing sets and master sets. Apprentice TCG Collector villagers offer small binders with a capacity of 150 card slots, while Master TCG Collector villagers offer large binders that have 300 tcg card slots.

(Future plans) 
- Bug Fixes

I plan on fixing the glitched texture lines that are visible on cards with the enchant glint. I also plan to fix the direction TCG Collector villagers hold the Pokémon TCG blocks.  
- TCG Battles 

A dedicated play mat system is planned for future updates. Until then, players can use item frames and storage blocks to create their own tabletop battle setups. I also plan on adding Theme decks and battle decks 

- Coins and Sleeves

Coins would be obtained from booster products or elite trainer boxes and will serve as decorative collectibles, with potential future use in TCG Battles. Sleeves will allow players to protect and customize the appearance of their cards while also adding an extra layer of rarity to their collection.
- Card grading

Cards would be able to receive item durability conditions acting as grades. Higher grades will increase a card's value and make rare pulls even more collectible.
- International Language Sets

Future updates may include Pokémon TCG sets from other regions and languages, including Japanese expansions and promotional cards.
